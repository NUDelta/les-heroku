Notification Preferences

In this section, please tell us if you would like to be notified by LES if it knows certain information about a Gym nearby. These responses will be used by LES to determine when to notify you about Gyms.

If you respond No to a prompt below, you will not be notified by LES when it knows that specific piece of information about a nearby Gym. However, you will still be notified about prompts that you respond Yes to even if they are below one you said No to.

For example, if you respond:
LES knows that a nearby gym has free weights available. No
LES knows that a nearby gym has free weights with benches available. Yes
then LES would not notify you about free weights generally but would notify you about free weights with benches.
