Notification Preferences

In this section, please tell us if you would like to be notified by LES if it knows certain information about a Free Food event nearby. These responses will be used by LES to determine when to notify you about Free Food events.

If you respond No to a prompt below, you will not be notified by LES when it knows that specific piece of information about Free Food events. However, you will still be notified about prompts that you respond Yes to even if they are below one you said No to.

For example, if you respond:
LES knows that there's a free food event with savory snacks happening nearby. No
LES knows that there's a free food event with pizza happening nearby. Yes
then LES would not notify you about savory snacks generally but would notify you about pizza.
