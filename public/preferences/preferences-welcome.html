Understanding What You Like

As users contribute data to the LES community, we provide the data back to you when we think it's something that you might be interested in (for example, private tables near windows at coffee shops).

On the next few pages, we will ask you questions about what you would want to be notified about and how likely you would be to go if a certain amount of information existed. It will take about 10-15 minutes to finish this process. Afterwards, your account will be completely setup and we will provide you with an installation link for LES.
